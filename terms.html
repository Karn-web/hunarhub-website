💼 Platform Description

HunarHub is a freelancing platform that connects clients with freelancers. We provide tools for job posting, proposals, communication, and secure payment processing.

💰 Payments & Fees

Platform Fee

HunarHub charges a 14% platform fee on all transactions. This fee is deducted from the total payment made by the client.

Payment Flow

  • Clients pay 50% advance to start work
  • Remaining 50% is paid upon completion
  • Funds are held securely until work is approved
  • Freelancers can withdraw to their UPI ID

Withdrawals

  • Minimum withdrawal amount: ₹500
  • Maximum daily withdrawal: ₹50,000
  • Processing time: 24-48 hours

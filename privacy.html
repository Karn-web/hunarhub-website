Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2024

🔓 Introduction

Welcome to HunarHub. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

📋 Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile information and skills
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay)
  • UPI ID for withdrawals
  • Device information and IP address

Usage Data

  • Job postings and proposals
  • Chat messages between users
  • Transaction history
  • App usage analytics

🔧 How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our freelancing platform services
  • To process payments and withdrawals securely
  • To facilitate communication between clients and freelancers
  • To improve our app and user experience
  • To send important notifications and updates
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security

🔐 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Payment processing is handled by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant provider
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures

👥 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Razorpay - Payment processing
  • Firebase - Authentication, database, and hosting
  • Google Play Services - App distribution

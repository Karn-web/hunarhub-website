Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

Introduction

HunarHub ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using HunarHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name: Your full name for profile identification.
  • Email Address: For account creation and communication.
  • Phone Number: For OTP verification and account security.
  • Profile Photo: Optional image for your public profile.
  • UPI ID: For processing withdrawals (freelancers only).
  • PAN Card: For tax compliance and KYC verification.

Usage Information

  • Job Postings: Content you post as a client.
  • Proposals: Applications you submit as a freelancer.
  • Chat Messages: Communications between users.
  • Payment History: Transaction records for wallet operations.
  • Ratings & Reviews: Feedback you provide after job completion.

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address and location data
  • App usage statistics and crash logs

How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account.
  • Service Delivery: To connect clients with freelancers and process payments.
  • Communication: To send notifications, updates, and support messages.
  • Security: To detect fraud, prevent abuse, and ensure platform safety.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance our Service.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with Indian laws and regulations.

Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay for secure payment processing.
  • Cloud Services: Firebase (Google) for data storage and authentication.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or court order.
  • Other Users: Public profile information visible to all users.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Firebase Authentication with secure token-based access
  • Firestore security rules restricting unauthorized access
  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

Your Rights

Under Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • File a complaint with the Data Protection Authority
